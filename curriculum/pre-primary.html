Pre-Primary Education

Our Pre-Primary program is designed to provide young children with a strong foundation for their educational journey. We cater to children aged 2.5 to 6 years, offering a nurturing and stimulating environment where they can learn and grow.

Our Classes

  • Play Group (PP1): Ages 2.5 - 3 years
  • Pre-Primary 1 (PP1): Ages 3 - 4 years
  • Pre-Primary 2 (PP2): Ages 4 - 5 years
  • Pre-Primary 3 (PP3): Ages 5 - 6 years

Learning Areas

Our Pre-Primary curriculum covers the following learning areas as per the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC):

  • Language Activities: Developing communication skills through stories, songs, and conversations
  • Mathematical Activities: Introducing numbers, shapes, patterns, and basic counting
  • Environmental Activities: Exploring the world around them and understanding their environment
  • Psychomotor and Creative Activities: Developing motor skills through play, art, and movement
  • Religious Education Activities: Building moral values and spiritual awareness

Our Approach

We believe that young children learn best through play. Our play-based approach to learning allows children to:

  • Explore and discover at their own pace
  • Develop social skills through interaction with peers
  • Build confidence and independence
  • Express creativity through art, music, and drama
  • Develop physical skills through active play

Our Facilities

Our Pre-Primary section features:

  • Bright, colorful, and child-friendly classrooms
  • Age-appropriate learning materials and toys
  • Safe outdoor play areas
  • Clean and hygienic facilities
  • Dedicated and caring teachers

Daily Schedule

A typical day at our Pre-Primary section includes:

  • Morning assembly and prayer
  • Indoor learning activities
  • Snack time
  • Outdoor play and physical activities
  • Creative activities (art, music, drama)
  • Story time
  • Lunch and rest time
  • Afternoon activities
