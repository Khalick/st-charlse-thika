Developing Well-Rounded Students

St. Charles School Thika is committed to assisting students achieve their full potential. We encourage students to participate in exciting extracurricular activities including swimming, sports and creative arts. We have an attractive and well-maintained campus that provides ample space for various activities.

Sports

Physical fitness and sportsmanship are key components of our educational philosophy. Our sports program includes:

  • Swimming: We have a well-maintained swimming pool where students learn to swim and compete
  • Football: Both boys and girls football teams compete in local tournaments
  • Netball: A popular sport among our students with regular training and competitions
  • Athletics: Track and field events including running, jumping, and throwing
  • Volleyball: Team sport promoting coordination and teamwork
  • Basketball: Indoor sport available for interested students
  • Table Tennis: Available for students to develop hand-eye coordination

Creative Arts

We nurture creativity and artistic expression through various programs:

  • Music: Choir, instrumental music, and music theory
  • Dance: Traditional and contemporary dance
  • Drama: Acting, public speaking, and theatrical performances
  • Art: Drawing, painting, and crafts
  • Poetry: Creative writing and recitation

Clubs and Societies

We offer various clubs to cater to different interests:

  • Debate Club: Developing public speaking and critical thinking skills
  • Science Club: Exploring scientific concepts through experiments
  • Environmental Club: Promoting environmental conservation
  • Drama Club: Theatrical performances and acting skills
  • Scouts and Girl Guides: Leadership and life skills development
  • Reading Club: Fostering a love for reading
  • Mathematics Club: Problem-solving and mathematical exploration

Competitions and Events

Our students participate in various competitions throughout the year:

  • Kenya Music and Drama Festivals
  • Inter-school sports competitions
  • Science and technology fairs
  • Public speaking competitions
  • Art exhibitions
  • Cultural days and celebrations

Benefits of Extracurricular Activities

Participation in extracurricular activities helps students to:

  • Develop teamwork and collaboration skills
  • Build confidence and self-esteem
  • Discover and nurture hidden talents
  • Learn time management and discipline
  • Make new friends and build social skills
  • Stay physically fit and healthy
  • Develop leadership qualities
  • Express creativity and imagination
