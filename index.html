Welcome to St. Charles School Thika

Growing in Knowledge & Character

Welcome to St. Charles School Thika. Thank you for visiting our website and taking an interest in our school. We hope that our website gives you insight into our commitment to providing a supportive learning environment that encourages students to achieve their full potential.

Our school is a well-known top performing academic institution that not only offers quality education, but an array of non-academic programs that identify and nurture talent. We have dedicated teachers who are committed to helping every child succeed.

Our History

St. Charles School Thika was first conceived as a nursery school in 1998 with just three kindergarten students, but demand for it to expand to a primary school saw the school open its doors...

Who We Are

St. Charles School Thika is a private primary school focused on providing excellent education services to students between the ages of two and half to fourteen years.

Mission & Vision

To provide quality, holistic education that nurtures academic excellence, moral values, and life skills in a safe and supportive environment.
