Welcome to St. Charles School Thika

St. Charles School Thika welcomes all students regardless of gender, race, religion or culture. We accept students for admission throughout the year. The admissions procedures are slightly different for new students and transfer students.

Admission Requirements

For New Students (Pre-Primary)

  • Child must be at least 2.5 years old
  • Birth certificate (original and copy)
  • Two passport-size photographs
  • Immunization records
  • Parent/Guardian ID (copy)

For Transfer Students (Primary)

  • Transfer letter from previous school
  • Latest school report card
  • Birth certificate (original and copy)
  • Two passport-size photographs
  • Parent/Guardian ID (copy)
  • Clearance certificate from previous school

Admission Process

  1. Inquiry: Visit the school or contact us to learn more about our programs and facilities.
  2. School Tour: Schedule a tour of our campus to see our facilities and meet our staff.
  3. Application: Complete and submit the admission application form with all required documents.
  4. Assessment: Transfer students may be required to take an assessment test.
  5. Interview: A brief interview with the parent/guardian and student.
  6. Admission Decision: You will be notified of the admission decision.
  7. Registration: Pay the required fees and complete the registration process.

School Fees

Our school fees are competitive and cover:

  • Tuition fees
  • Learning materials and textbooks
  • Extracurricular activities
  • Swimming lessons
  • School meals (lunch and snacks)
  • Medical first aid

For detailed fee structure, please contact our admissions office or visit the school.

School Calendar

Our academic year follows the Kenyan school calendar with three terms:

  • Term 1: January - April
  • Term 2: May - August
  • Term 3: September - November
