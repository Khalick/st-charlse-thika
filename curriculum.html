Our Approach to Learning

We believe in a learner-centered, competency-based approach to education. Our teaching methods are designed to:

  • Encourage active participation and critical thinking
  • Develop problem-solving and decision-making skills
  • Foster creativity and innovation
  • Build strong communication skills
  • Promote collaboration and teamwork
  • Nurture individual talents and interests

Academic Excellence

St. Charles School Thika has a strong track record of academic excellence. Our students consistently perform well in national examinations, with many achieving top marks in Thika West Sub-County and beyond.

Our success is built on:

  • Qualified and experienced teachers
  • Small class sizes for personalized attention
  • Regular assessments and progress monitoring
  • Strong parent-teacher partnerships
  • Modern learning resources and facilities
