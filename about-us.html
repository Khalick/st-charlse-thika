About Us

Home / About Us

St. Charles School Thika is a Private educational institution committed to providing high quality, learner-centered, competency-based education to children between the ages of 2.5 to 14 years.

Our Commitment

We are dedicated to nurturing young minds and helping them achieve their full potential. Our school provides a supportive learning environment that encourages academic excellence, moral development, and the cultivation of life skills.

What Sets Us Apart

  • Experienced and dedicated teaching staff
  • Modern learning facilities and resources
  • Comprehensive curriculum following the Kenyan education system
  • Strong focus on character development and moral values
  • Wide range of extracurricular activities
  • Safe and nurturing environment
  • Consistent top performance in national examinations
