About St. Charles School Thika

St. Charles School Thika is a private primary school focused on providing excellent education services to students between the ages of two and half to fourteen years. The school is located East of Thika town in Makongeni Estate, Phase 13, opposite Gresta University, off Garissa Road.

We are a well-known top performing academic institution that not only offers quality education, but an array of non-academic programs that identify and nurture talent. We have dedicated teachers who are committed to helping every child succeed.

Our Identity

At St. Charles School Thika, we believe that every child is unique and deserves the opportunity to develop their full potential. Our school provides a nurturing environment where students can grow academically, socially, emotionally, and spiritually.

Our Values

  • Excellence: We strive for the highest standards in everything we do
  • Integrity: We uphold honesty and strong moral principles
  • Respect: We treat everyone with dignity and consideration
  • Responsibility: We take ownership of our actions and their outcomes
  • Compassion: We show kindness and care for others
  • Teamwork: We work together to achieve common goals

Our Community

Our school community is made up of dedicated teachers, supportive parents, and enthusiastic students. Together, we create a positive learning environment that fosters growth, creativity, and success.

We welcome students from all backgrounds, regardless of gender, race, religion, or culture. Our diverse community enriches the learning experience and prepares students to thrive in an interconnected world.

Our Facilities

St. Charles School Thika boasts modern facilities designed to support effective learning:

  • Spacious and well-ventilated classrooms
  • Well-stocked library
  • Computer laboratory
  • Science laboratory
  • Sports facilities including a swimming pool
  • Playground and outdoor learning spaces
  • Clean and hygienic dining facilities
