Our History

Home / About Us / Our History

The Beginning

St. Charles School Thika was first conceived as a nursery school in 1998 with just three kindergarten students. The school was founded with a vision to provide quality education to children in Thika and the surrounding areas.

What started as a small nursery school quickly gained recognition for its commitment to excellence and the personal attention given to each child. The demand from parents for the school to expand led to the decision to open a primary school section.

Growth and Development

As word spread about the quality of education at St. Charles School, enrollment steadily increased. The school expanded its facilities to accommodate the growing number of students while maintaining the close-knit community feel that parents appreciated.

Over the years, the school has invested in modern infrastructure, including:

  • Construction of additional classrooms
  • Establishment of a computer laboratory
  • Building of a science laboratory
  • Development of sports facilities
  • Construction of a swimming pool
  • Expansion of the library

Academic Excellence

St. Charles School Thika has consistently performed well in national examinations, earning recognition as one of the top schools in Thika West Sub-County. Our students have gone on to join prestigious secondary schools across Kenya.

In 2022, the school was awarded trophies for being the best performing school in KCPE in Thika West Sub-County, a testament to the dedication of our teachers and the hard work of our students.

Looking to the Future

As we continue to grow, St. Charles School Thika remains committed to its founding principles of providing quality, holistic education. We are constantly evolving to meet the changing needs of education while maintaining the values that have made us successful.

Today, St. Charles School Thika stands as a beacon of educational excellence in the region, continuing to shape young minds and prepare students for a bright future.
