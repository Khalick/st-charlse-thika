2023 Class 8 Performance

Home / News / 2023 Class 8 Performance
February 27, 2024 Catherine Njeri

Congratulations Class of 2023!

We are thrilled to announce the outstanding performance of our Class of 2023 in the Kenya Certificate of Primary Education (KCPE) examinations. Our students have once again demonstrated their dedication to academic excellence and have made us all proud.

The results reflect the hard work of our students, the dedication of our teachers, and the support of our parents. We are grateful to everyone who contributed to this success.

Highlights of Our Performance

  • Top student scored above 400 marks
  • Over 90% of our students scored above 300 marks
  • Significant improvement in all subjects
  • Several students admitted to top national schools

Message to Our Graduates

To our dear Class of 2023, this is just the beginning of your educational journey. As you move on to secondary school, remember the values and lessons you learned at St. Charles School Thika. Continue to work hard, stay focused, and always strive for excellence.

We wish you all the best in your future endeavors. You will always be part of the St. Charles family!

Thank You

We extend our heartfelt gratitude to:

  • Our dedicated teachers who worked tirelessly to prepare our students
  • Parents who supported their children throughout the year
  • The students themselves for their hard work and determination
  • The school administration for providing the necessary resources
